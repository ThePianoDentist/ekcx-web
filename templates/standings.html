Points System

Points are awarded based on finishing position:

  • 1st: 100 points
  • 2nd: 94 points
  • 3rd: 90 points
  • 4th: 86 points
  • 5th: 83 points
  • 6th: 80 points
  • 7th: 78 points
  • 8th: 76 points
  • 9th: 74 points
  • 10th: 72 points
  • 11th: 70 points
  • 12th: 69 points
  • 13th+: Decrease by 1 point per position

Note: If a rider competes in more than one race, their lowest score is dropped from the total.
