Bettshanger Park Cycling Challenges

Unofficial records of cycling challenges around the tarmac track at Bettshanger Park. Email any submissions to thepianodentist@protonmail.com. Remember to be careful when cycling round the track as families will be obliviously sauntering around. Also pay special attention at the junctions with the Mountain Bike trails. I expect you to ride sensibly given it's a public park and take no liability for if you're an idiot. Good luck :)
