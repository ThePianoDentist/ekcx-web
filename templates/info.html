Standings Calculation

  • Each rider's lowest score is dropped from their total, including absences (which count as 0 points).
  • Final standings are calculated using the total points after dropping the lowest score.
  • Example: If a rider wins 4 races (100 points each = 400 points) and misses 1 race (0 points), their lowest score (0) is dropped, giving them 400 points. If they win 4 races and also get a 2nd (i.e. 494 total points), their lowest score (94) is dropped, also giving them 400 points.
