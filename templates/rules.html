Points System

Points are awarded based on finishing position in each race:

Position Points
1st 100
2nd 94
3rd 90
4th 86
5th 83
6th 80
7th 78
8th 76
9th 74
10th 72
11th 70
12th 69
13th+ Decrease by 1 point per position

Standings Calculation

  • Each rider's lowest score is dropped from their total, including absences (which count as 0 points).
  • Final standings are calculated using the total points after dropping the lowest score.
  • Example: If a rider wins 4 races (100 points each = 400 points) and misses 1 race (0 points), their lowest score (0) is dropped, giving them 400 points. If they win all 5 races (100 points each = 500 points), their lowest score (100) is dropped, also giving them 400 points.
  • If a rider has only competed in one race, all points count toward their total.
